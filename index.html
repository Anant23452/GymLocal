Good To Know

Plan FAQ

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard; the change applies on your next renewal.

Is there a joining fee?

No joining fee. The price you see is the price you pay.

What happens when my membership expires?

Your dashboard flags renewals 7 days out. After expiry, gym access pauses until you renew — your data and history stay intact.
